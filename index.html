General Settings

Display the number of active tasks next to each category in the sidebar.

  • Create Task: Press N or click the small + button next to any category name.
  • Priority Pinning: Click the icon on a card to pin it to the top of your list.
  • Smart Tags: Tasks are automatically flagged as Urgent or Upcoming based on your settings.
  • Filtering:
    • Sidebar: Click "View Categories" to see your lists.
    • Search Bar: Type to build custom filters (e.g., "Goals" + "Urgent").
  • Formatting: Use Markdown in descriptions: **bold**, *italics*, [link](url).
  • Data & Privacy: All data is stored locally in your browser. This means the site will not keep your data in a Private Browser or if you clear your cookies. Use the Export button in the header to back up your data to a file. and the Import button to transfer your data from another browser or device.
