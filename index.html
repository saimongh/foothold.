Settings

  • Create Task: Press N or click the small + button next to any category name.
  • Smart Tags: Tasks are automatically flagged as Urgent or Upcoming based on your settings in the "Smart Tags" tab.
  • Filtering:
    • Sidebar: Click a category to view only that list.
    • Search Bar: Type to build custom filters (e.g., "Goals" + "Urgent").
  • Mobile Actions:
    • Reorder Cards: Tap & Hold a card for 0.5s until it lifts, then drag.
    • Reorder Categories: Drag using the grip icon (::) on the left.
  • Formatting: Use Markdown in descriptions: **bold**, *italics*, [link](url).
  • Data & Privacy: All data is stored locally in your browser. Use the Export button in the header to back up your data to a file.
